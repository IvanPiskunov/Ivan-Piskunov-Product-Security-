Teaching principles

  • Fundamentals first: students should understand how systems and risks actually work before they memorize tools.
  • Industry relevance: learning should map to real delivery environments, not just exams.
  • Ethics and responsibility: technical skill without ethical framing is incomplete.
  • Career readiness: security education should help students become useful, credible, and employable.
