Long bio

Ivan Piskunov is a Product Security leader and practice-led educator with more than 15 years of cybersecurity experience spanning secure SDLC, AppSec, DevSecOps, Cloud & Platform Security, governance, vulnerability management, and regulated engineering environments. Across his industry path, he has worked from hands-on technical roles to broader security leadership, helping teams build structured security workflows, improve signal quality, connect security with delivery realities, and translate risk into operational action. In addition to his industry track, he has developed a strong educator profile through university lecturing, mentoring, authored courses, books, brochures, GitHub learning repositories, challenge design, and public-facing technical writing. Rather than presenting as a classic research academic, he is best understood as an industry-built educator who brings real operating depth into applied learning environments. He is open to Product Security leadership, Lecturer, Professor of Practice-aligned, executive education, curriculum-building, and academic-industry collaboration opportunities.
