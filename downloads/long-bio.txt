Rather than presenting himself as a classic research academic, he is best positioned as an industry-built educator who brings real operating depth into applied learning environments. He is open to Product Security leadership, Lecturer, Professor of Practice-aligned, executive education, curriculum design, and academic-industry collaboration opportunities.
